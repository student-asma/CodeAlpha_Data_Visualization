# Customer Shopping Behavior Data Visualization

## Project Overview

This project focuses on transforming customer shopping data into meaningful visual insights using Python visualization libraries. The goal is to present customer behavior, purchasing patterns, and business trends through clear and informative charts and graphs.

## Objective

The main objective of this project is to:

* Convert raw data into visual representations
* Identify customer behavior patterns
* Discover trends and relationships within the dataset
* Support data-driven decision making through visual storytelling

## Dataset Information

The dataset contains customer shopping behavior data, including:

* Customer demographics
* Product categories
* Purchase amounts
* Subscription status
* Shipping preferences
* Review ratings

## Tools and Libraries Used

* Python
* Pandas
* Matplotlib
* Seaborn
* Jupyter Notebook

## Visualizations Created

### 1. Revenue by Gender

A bar chart was used to compare total revenue generated by male and female customers.

**Insight:** Female customers generated slightly higher revenue than male customers.

### 2. Product Category Analysis

A visualization showing the most frequently purchased product categories.

**Insight:** Clothing products were purchased more frequently than other categories.

### 3. Subscription Status Impact

A comparison of spending behavior between subscribers and non-subscribers.

**Insight:** Subscribers spent more on average than non-subscribers.

### 4. Age Distribution

A histogram illustrating the distribution of customer ages.

**Insight:** Most customers belonged to middle-age groups.

### 5. Shipping Type Analysis

A comparison of average purchase amounts across different shipping methods.

**Insight:** Customers using express shipping showed higher average spending.

## Key Findings

* Female customers contribute slightly more revenue.
* Clothing is the most popular product category.
* Subscription programs positively influence customer spending.
* Express shipping customers tend to spend more.
* Visual analytics makes business trends easier to understand.

## Conclusion

Data visualization plays a crucial role in uncovering hidden patterns and communicating insights effectively. Through this project, customer shopping behavior was analyzed visually, enabling better understanding of purchasing trends and business opportunities.
